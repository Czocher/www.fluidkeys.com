About us

Paul and Ian keep themselves busy fighting internet surveillance and censorship.

They run a Tor node and maintain a bot that reminds you if you’re PGP key is about to expire.

Previously they’ve run cryptoparties at the UK’s largest consumer Co-op and helped setup a gym that puts people’s physical energy to use in their local communities.

We’re building a better way to do PGP sign up to hear more.
